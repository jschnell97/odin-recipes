Farro With Sweet Potato and Feta

But soft! What be these bronzed pearls of wonder? Feast your eyes and mouth upon mine ancient grain.

Ingredients

  • Farro
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Feta
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Boil Farro
  2. Roast sweet potatoes
  3. Mix with olive oil
  4. Top with feta
