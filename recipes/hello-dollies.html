Hello Dollies

Hark! Gaze thine eyes upon mine most decadent nourishment of the soul. Like sands in the hour glass are mine fanciful layers of cocoa rivers atop fine granules of graham, finished with crisps of the most enjoyable tropical fruit.

As tears flow through mine eyes, I lift one treat of utmost satiety toward mine face hole. As it were to enter mine hole, the salivatory excitement which ensued must have only been compared to one flood of Biblical proportion. The taste, nay, the Mazurka which my tastebuds so joyously performed in mine hole was overwhelming. Excelsior!

Ingredients

  • Cocoa Rivers of Plenty
  • The Finest Tropical Flakes
  • Graham toasts

Steps

  1. Combine Ingredients
  2. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes
